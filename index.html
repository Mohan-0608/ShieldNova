    FAQ

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What makes ShieldNova AI different from traditional antivirus?+

    Traditional antivirus relies on known threat signatures. ShieldNova AI uses machine learning and behavioral analytics to detect unknown, zero-day threats in real time — catching attacks signature-based tools miss entirely.

    How long does setup take?+

    Most businesses are fully operational within 24–48 hours. Our onboarding team handles the integration and configuration — no deep technical expertise required on your end.

    Is my data safe and private?+

    Absolutely. We use AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. All data is processed within compliant, region-locked infrastructure. We never sell or share your data with third parties.

    Do you support small businesses and startups?+

    Yes — our Starter plan is purpose-built for small teams. Cybersecurity shouldn't be a luxury. We've priced our plans to make enterprise-grade protection accessible at every stage of growth.

    Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?+

    Yes. Plans are billed monthly with no lock-in. You can upgrade instantly and downgrade at the end of your billing cycle. Our team will guide you through any changes.

    What compliance standards do you support?+

    We align with ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, and India's IT Act. Our Business plan includes compliance reporting to help your audit and regulatory needs.
